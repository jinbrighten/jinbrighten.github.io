I am an Integrated MS/PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. [Youngki Lee](https://youngkilee.blogspot.com/) in the [Human-Centered Computer Systems Lab](https://hcs.snu.ac.kr). I received my BS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University in 2024.

My research builds **mobile systems for embodied interaction** — where perception must operate inside ongoing physical actions on resource-constrained devices. I am especially interested in how the structure of physical interaction (contact, affordance, manipulation) can serve as a runtime constraint that validates and refines learning-based perception. My current project explores interaction-constrained 6-DoF object pose tracking during hand manipulation.

Previously, I was a visiting researcher at Purdue University (Aug–Oct 2025), where I scoped a collaboration on high-fidelity teleinteraction with the Purdue Spatial Computing Hub and formulated the research problem behind my ongoing work with Prof. Younghyun Kim's group.
